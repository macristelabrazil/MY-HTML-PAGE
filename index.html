The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal.
It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and is commonly referred to as the domestic cat or house cat to distinguish it from the wild members of the family.

Cats communicate through various vocalizations, body language, and behaviors such as purring, meowing, hissing, and rubbing against objects or people. They are generally known for their independent nature, but they can also show affection and seek human companionship.>
